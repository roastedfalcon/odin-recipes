Hamburger

Description

This is the easiest all-purpose recipe for perfect hamburger patties every time! These delicious homemade hamburgers are ready in less than 30 minutes and are a must-make for your next cookout.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground chuck
  • 1/2 crushed saltine crackers or bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. In large mixing bowl, add in ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and pepper. Mix by hand until the meat mixture is smooth.
  2. Press meat down in bowl into an even disk. Use a knife to cut and divide the patty mixture into 6 1/3 pound grill or skillet patties.
  3. Set out baking sheet, lined with wax paper or foil. One at a time, gather the patty mix and press firmly into patties. Shape them slightly larger than the buns to account for shrinkage when cooking. Using a spopn, press a dent in the center of each patty so they don't puff up as they cook.
  4. Preheat grill to medium heat then grill patties for 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Stack hot patties on hamburger buns, and serve with your favorite hamburger toppings.
